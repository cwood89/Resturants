Los Angeles

Los Angeles is know for many great things, but it's food scene is arguably one of the best in the country.

Here is a list of the top five:

  1. Roscoe's House of Chicken and Waffles

    Roscoes as the name suggests is known for it's chicken and waffles but they also have great mac and cheese among other items.

    See Menu

  2. In and Out Burger

    In and Out is the most popular burger chain in California, and for good reason. Using only fresh ingredients everything is made in house. Check out their secret menu!

    See Menu

  3. Versailles Cuban Food

    Versailles is a cuban food resturant with the best plantains money can buy! Don't forget to order some when you visit!

    See Menu

  4. Dan's Super Subs

    These sandwiches are in a class of their own. Their special sauce completely transforms the sub.

    See Menu

  5. Leo's Tacos Truck

    Located on the corner of Venice and LaBrea you wouldn't expect the get the best taco you've ever tried from a truck, but looks can be deceiving. They also have amazing burritos!

    See Menu
