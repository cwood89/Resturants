Burrito Bowl

Ingredient Amount
Chicken Breast 10 ounces
Corn 1 can
Bell Peppers 3 whole
Gaucamole 1/4 Cup
Salsa 1/4 Cup
Black Beans 1 can
Lime .5 oz
Onion 1 whole
Rice 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Season and Grill Chicken Breast and chop into strips.
  2. Sautee peppers and onions untill tender.
  3. Cook Rice and add lime juice if desired.
  4. Heat Beans and Corn.
  5. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  6. Top with Salsa and Quacamole.
  7. Enjoy!
