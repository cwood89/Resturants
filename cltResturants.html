Charlotte

Charlotte is an upcoming city in the food scene. There's plenty of great places to eat.

Here is a list of the top five:

  1. Chili's

    Chilli's is a great place if you cant make up your mind, their options range from their famous ribs to burritos. There's always something new to try.

    See Menu

  2. P.F Chang's

    P.F Chang's is a chinese food spot that has a great vegetarian menu as well as great portions. You wont leave hungry!

    See Menu

  3. Mellow Mushroom

    Mellow Mushroom is a pizza place that doesn't charge extra for vegan cheese. Who doens't love pizza!

    See Menu

  4. Viva Chicken

    With a great atmosphere, Viva Chicken is a Peruvian food place. They're nown for their rotissere chicken, but that doesnt mean their other dishes aren't amazing.

    See Menu

  5. Maggiano's

    If you like Italian food, Maggiano's is the place to be. Don't worry if you have a gluten allergy, they have gluten free options!

    See Menu
